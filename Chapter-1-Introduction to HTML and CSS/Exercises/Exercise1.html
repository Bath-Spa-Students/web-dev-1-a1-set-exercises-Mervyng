Noodle Recipe

Components:

  • 250g noodles (e.g., spaghetti, penne, fusilli)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional) for serving

Steps:

  1. Boil a large pot of salted water. Cook the noodles as per the instructions, until done. Drain and keep aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat the olive oil. Add the minced garlic and chopped onion. Cook until soft and fragrant, around 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add the tomato sauce, dried basil, and dried oregano to the pan. Season with salt and pepper. Simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Add the cooked noodles to the pan with the sauce. Mix well to ensure the noodles are evenly covered.
  5. Remove from heat. Serve hot with an optional sprinkle of Parmesan cheese.
