Plot

In a rural town in the 1980s, a group of misfit teens stumbles upon a government experiment gone wrong. They must navigate dark secrets, face terrifying creatures, and uncover the truth behind a parallel dimension's invasion to rescue their missing friend and save their town from destruction.

Cast

  • Winona Ryder as Joyce Byers
  • David Harbour as Chief Jim Hopper
  • Finn Wolfhard as Mike Wheeler
  • Millie Bobby Brown as Eleven

Characters

  • Mike Wheeler
  • Dustin Henderson

Mike Wheeler

Mike Wheeler, a thoughtful and determined leader, leads his friends with loyalty and bravery, driven to find his missing friend. Skeptical at first, he bonds deeply with Eleven, revealing his compassionate and protective side. Evolving from cautious to courageous, he risks everything for his loved ones.

Dustin Henderson

Dustin Henderson, charismatic and witty, overcomes challenges with confidence and humor. His scientific curiosity aids the group, offering key insights. Valuing friendship deeply, he stands as a loyal and supportive companion, particularly in confronting supernatural threats. Dustin's charm, intelligence, and resilience solidify his role as an essential team member.

Lucas Sinclair

Lucas Sinclair is a pragmatic and fiercely loyal friend, often serving as the voice of reason within the group. With his sharp instincts and determination, he navigates challenges with a level-headed approach. Lucas's unwavering loyalty and courage make him a vital asset in facing the supernatural threats lurking in their town.

Eleven

Eleven is a mysterious and enigmatic girl with psychokinetic abilities, escaping from a government laboratory. Despite her past trauma, she forms a deep bond with Mike and his friends. As she explores her powers, Eleven grapples with her identity and learns to harness her abilities for good.
